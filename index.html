About

Motion that moves
brands forward

I'm Malek Bouzgarrou, a motion designer with a broad creative range — from cinematic logo animations to fluid UI/UX transitions that make digital products feel alive. I craft motion with intention: every easing curve, every timing choice is purposeful.

My work spans logo reveals, social media content designed to stop the scroll, and SaaS/app interface animations that enhance user experience through storytelling motion. Whether a brand needs a signature animated identity or a product team needs micro-interactions that feel premium, I bring both aesthetic vision and technical precision.
